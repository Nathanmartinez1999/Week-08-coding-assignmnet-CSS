Paul McCartney Solo Carrer

Paul McCartney was the first of the beatles to begin thier solo carrer, Paul would release his first solo album almost exactly 10 days after the release of the final beatles album "Let It Be". Paul would go on to form a rock band in the 1970s that included his first wife linda on piano and backing vocals the band was named "wings" during the better part of a decade from 1970 to 1980 wings would tour across america preforming songs from pauls solo albums and even melodies of old beatles songs, the group would break up following the departure of paul in 1980. Paul is the second still living member of the beatles and even today more then half a decade since the start of his solo carrer pual continues to release new music and tour across the globe

    Wings Band Members

  • Paul McCartney, Lead vocals/Piano/Bass
  • Linda McCartney, Backing vocals/Piano
  • Denny Laine, Lead Guitar
  • Jimmy McCulloch, guitar/Piano
