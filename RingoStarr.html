Ringo Starr Solo Carrer

Ringo starr began his solo carrer very shortly after the breakup of the beatles, ringo would form his own band and would tour and record music under his own name since the creation of his first band since the beatles, Ringo is one of the two surviving beatles members still alive today, even into his early 80s ringo continues to record music and tour across the globe. during his solo carrer ringo would top the billboard top 100 chart two time with 1973s single "photograh" and 1973 hit "Your're Sixteen". seven more of ringos songs would reach the top 10. he would go on to sell over two million albums world wide.
