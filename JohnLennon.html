John Lennons Solo Carrer

Following the break-up of the beatles in 1970, john would only wait roughly 4 months after the breakup to release his first solo album, playing under his own name and being acompanied by the plastic ono band. which was john lennons touring and recording band, That group would consist of life long musical acquantance of johns and finially the band would include his wife Yono Oko who would share lead vocal roles with john. John lennon and the plastic ono band would preforme live across the world for almost an entire decade from 1970 till the death of john lennon in december of the year 1980
