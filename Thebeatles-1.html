The beatles are a rythem and blues band from liverpool, england. The band was formed in summner of the year 1960 by founding members John Lennon, Paul McCartney, George Harrison and Ringo Starr. The beatles would spend the early years of thier live playing days in the undergrounds and pubs of the local liverpool area, All four members of the band have been playing musical instraments since thier early teenage years. all menbers of the band were in thier early twenties by the formation of the beatles following two years of steady touring around liverpool and constant persistance the group would release thier first charting single titled "love me do" the song would release on october 10th 1963 and peak at number 41 on the charts, the success of love me do as well as increased attendance at live shows the beatles would soon be offerd a record deal. The beatles were signed to EMI records in the summer of 1963. This would mark the offical creation of the beatles music carrer.
